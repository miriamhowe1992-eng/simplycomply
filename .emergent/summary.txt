<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
Create a UK-based SaaS web platform called SimplyComply. This platform is a business compliance portal allowing UK businesses to sign up, select their business type and industry, and access all required compliance policies, procedures, templates, and guidance relevant to their sector.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Target Audience:** UK businesses only.
-   **Sectors Supported:** Multiple sectors like dental practices, healthcare, construction, hospitality, retail, education, etc.
-   **Core Offerings:** Industry-specific compliance packs including Health & Safety policies, GDPR, Staff Handbooks, Risk Assessments, and regulatory guidance (e.g., CQC, HSE).
-   **Platform Features:**
    -   User account sign-up and login (JWT-based).
    -   Business profile setup (industry, size).
    -   Auto-generated compliance checklist based on industry.
    -   Document library with download/customization features.
    -   Subscription-based access (monthly/annual via Stripe).
    -   Employee management section to track individual compliance (e.g., DBS checks).
    -   Version control and update notifications for policies.
    -   Dashboard showing overall compliance status.
    -   Annual review reminders and alerts.
-   **User Roles:** Business Owner/Manager and SimplyComply Admin.
-   **Design Style:** Professional, clean, and reassuring with a blue/teal/neutral color palette.

**User's preferred language**: English

**what currently exists?**
